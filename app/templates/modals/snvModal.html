Optional Field: Single Nucleotide Variants

If providing single nucleotide variants (SNVs) for upload, they should follow the guidelines set by either the TCGA or NCI's Mutation Annotation Format (MAF). We recommend MuTect and Oncotator to generate this file. The Molecular Oncology Almanac specifically requires the following columns:

Hugo_Symbol, NCBI_Build, Chromosome, Start_position, End_position, Reference_Allele, Tumor_Seq_Allele1, Tumor_Seq_Allele2, Variant_Classification, Protein_Change, Tumor_Sample_Barcode, Matched_Norm_Sample_Barcode, Annotation_Transcript,
t_ref_count, t_alt_count
