Optional Field: Single Nucleotide Variants from RNA

If providing single nucleotide variants from RNA (RNA SNVs) for upload, they should follow the guidelines set by the NCI's Mutation Annotation Format (MAF). We recommend running MuTect on RNA recalibrated BAMs using GATK Best Practices and Oncotator to generate this data. The Molecular Oncology Almanac specifically requires the following columns:

Hugo_Symbol, NCBI_Build, Chromosome, Start_position, End_position, Reference_Allele, Tumor_Seq_Allele1, Tumor_Seq_Allele2, Variant_Classification, Protein_Change, Tumor_Sample_Barcode, Matched_Norm_Sample_Barcode, Annotation_Transcript,
t_ref_count, t_alt_count
